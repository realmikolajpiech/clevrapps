How we think

Good software should feel obvious after someone has done the hard thinking.

  1. 01Find real friction.

    Start with a problem people already feel—not a feature looking for a reason.

  2. 02Make it feel simple.

    Careful product decisions disappear into an experience that feels natural.

  3. 03Ship, learn, sharpen.

    Momentum matters. Real users make products better than perfect mockups do.

Doso

Your health, effortlessly tracked.

Doso keeps medications, lab results and daily symptoms together, using AI to explain information in plain language and help you arrive at appointments better prepared.

What it does

Your health history.
Finally connected.

Health information is often fragmented across reminders, PDFs, notes and memory. Doso makes the day-to-day picture easier to track and easier to discuss with a professional.

  1. 01

    Medication schedules

    Organize what you take, when you take it and whether a scheduled dose has been completed.

  2. 02

    Lab results in plain language

    Upload results and get accessible explanations that help you prepare better questions for your doctor.

  3. 03

    Symptom history

    Log how you feel over time and preserve useful context for future appointments.

Inside the app

Health context,
when it matters.

Important: Doso does not replace professional medical advice. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional about your health and treatment.

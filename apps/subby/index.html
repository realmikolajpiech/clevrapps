Subby

Your subscriptions, under control.

Subby makes recurring spending visible. Track subscriptions and trials manually, see what renews next and understand where the money goes—without connecting a bank account.

What it does

Know what renews.
Before it renews.

Subscriptions are easy to start and surprisingly hard to remember. Subby keeps the important dates and totals in one private, straightforward dashboard.

  1. 01

    Renewal reminders

    Get advance notice before a subscription or free trial charges you again.

  2. 02

    Clear spending overview

    See monthly and yearly totals, category breakdowns and the services costing the most.

  3. 03

    Private by design

    Add subscriptions manually and stay in control without giving the app access to your bank account.

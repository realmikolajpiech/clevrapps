Trailo

Plan less. Travel more.

Describe the trip you want and Trailo builds a paced, ready-to-book itinerary with real flights, stays, official tickets and routes. Want it cheaper, slower or less crowded? Just ask.

What it does

From rough idea
to ready to go.

Tell Trailo where, when, with whom and what kind of trip you want. It turns that brief into a day-by-day plan with the travel options, timing and practical details already worked through.

  1. 01

    Paced, not packed

    Get realistic days built around opening hours, distances and routes—with room left to actually enjoy the place.

  2. 02

    Change anything by asking

    Ask for slower mornings, less walking, a cheaper day or a rainy-day option. Trailo reworks the whole plan in context.

  3. 03

    Goes with you

    Keep the itinerary, maps and tickets offline, navigate to what is next and share one live plan with everyone coming.

Trailo

Plan less. Travel more.

Trailo brings the moving parts of a trip into one clear plan—from flights and stays to tickets, maps and an itinerary for every hour that matters.

What it does

One trip.
One useful view.

Planning usually lives across tabs, confirmation emails, maps and notes. Trailo turns that scattered information into a plan you can understand at a glance and use while moving.

  1. 01

    Everything in context

    Keep transport, stays, reservations and places connected to the trip they belong to.

  2. 02

    Hour-by-hour itineraries

    Turn a collection of ideas into realistic days with a clear order and useful timing.

  3. 03

    Map-first orientation

    See the day geographically, understand what is nearby and spend less time retracing your steps.
